PROJECT

HOUSE OF THE RENT(HOR)

House hunting is a pain. If it is a hot sunny day or a stormy day or a just typical day, it is impossible to hunt for a house. To simplify the rental process for both homeowners and tenants, we came up with a rental management desktop application “House of the Rent”. This application will ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Objective:

  • Homeowners can create an account on the platform, where they can manage their rental properties and descriptions of their flats and set rental prices.
  • General people who are searching for houses can register for an account on the platform to browse the available apartments and descriptions.
  • The locations of the vacant houses will be in text format. If a tenant wants to visit a house, he can contact with the owner from the website contact info
